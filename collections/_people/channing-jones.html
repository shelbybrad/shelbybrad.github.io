Channing is reassurance personified.

She grew up in Troy, Michigan in the nineties, middle child of the Sleete family. Channing studied speech pathology and French in college, and makes a life of helping people as a speech language pathologist. Before her current hospital gig, she plied her trade at Berkley High School.
